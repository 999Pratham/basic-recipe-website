Chocolate Cake

Ingredients:

For Cake:
  • 1 and 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 and 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water
For Frosting (Optional):
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter (softened)
  • 2/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 3 cups powdered sugar
  • 1/3 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Procedure:

For Cake:
  1. Preheat Oven: Preheat your oven to 175°C (350°F). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.
  2. Mix Dry Ingredients: In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add Wet Ingredients: Add the eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla extract to the dry ingredients and beat with a hand mixer or whisk until well combined.
  4. Add Boiling Water: Carefully stir in the boiling water. The batter will be thin, but that’s normal.
  5. Bake: Divide the batter evenly between the prepared pans. Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  6. Cool: Allow the cakes to cool in the pans for about 10 minutes, then remove them from the pans and let them cool completely on a wire rack.
For Froasting(Optional):
  1. Mix Cocoa Powder: In a medium bowl, stir together the cocoa powder and softened butter until smooth.
  2. Add Powdered Sugar and Milk: Gradually add the powdered sugar and milk, alternating between the two, and beat until the frosting is smooth and creamy.
  3. Add Vanilla: Stir in the vanilla extract.
  4. Frost the Cake: Once the cakes are completely cooled, spread the frosting evenly over the top of one cake layer. Place the second layer on top and frost the entire cake.

Enjoy your homemade chocolate cake!

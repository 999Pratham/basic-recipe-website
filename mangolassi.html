Mango Lassi

Creamy Mango Lassi is a delicious blend of sweet mangoes, yogurt and flavored with cardamom. You can make this homemade version in 10 minutes!

Creamy Mango Lassi is easy to make at home with a few simple ingredients. I love making this lassi, especially during summers when sweet mangoes are in season. If you enjoy having mango lassi at Indian restaurants, you would love this homemade version just as much! All you need is 10 minutes to make this!

Recipe:

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe mango (peeled and chopped)
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/2 cup milk (adjust to desired consistency)
  • 2 tablespoons sugar (adjust to taste)
  • A pinch of cardamom powder (optional)
  • Ice cubes
  • Mint leaves for garnish (optional)

Instruction:

  1. Prepare the Mango: Peel and chop the ripe mango. Ensure it's sweet and juicy for the best flavor.
  2. Blend Ingredients: In a blender, add the chopped mango, yogurt, milk, sugar, and a pinch of cardamom powder.
  3. Add Ice: Add a few ice cubes to the blender for a chilled drink.
  4. Blend: Blend everything until smooth and creamy.
  5. Serve: Pour the mango lassi into glasses, garnish with mint leaves if you like, and enjoy!

This recipe can be adjusted to your taste by adding more sugar or reducing the milk for a thicker consistency.
